About CleanCity

Who We Are
And Why We Care.

CleanCity is a UK-wide initiative dedicated to transforming how cities handle waste and public spaces. We bring together communities, councils, and partners to build cleaner, greener neighbourhoods for everyone.

Our Vision

A future where every UK city is clean, safe, and environmentally conscious.

Our Mission

To reduce waste, improve urban living, and inspire climate-responsible behaviours.

Our Values

Community, transparency, sustainability, and long-term impact.
